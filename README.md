# latam-sales-forecasting-powerbi
This project simulates an end-to-end Commercial Intelligence and Demand Planning system for a fictional company operating across Latin America. The solution integrates data analysis, visualization, and business interpretation to support decision-making in areas such as:

-Sales performance
-Demand trends
-Channel effectiveness
-Product strategy

All data used in this project is fully synthetic and created for portfolio purposes.

🎯 Business Objective

Companies operating in multiple LATAM markets often lack:

Visibility into sales performance by country
Clarity on which products drive revenue
Understanding of channel effectiveness
Early signals of demand changes

This project addresses those challenges through a centralized analytics dashboard.

🧰 Tech Stack
Power BI → Data visualization & dashboarding
SQL → Data querying and aggregation
Python → Data cleaning and transformation
Excel → Business reporting simulation

- KEY INSIGHTS

Brazil leads total revenue, indicating it is the most critical market within the region.
Smartphones and Laptops contribute equally, reflecting a balanced product portfolio and reduced dependency risk.
Online channel dominates sales, highlighting the importance of digital commerce strategies.
Revenue shows a declining trend, suggesting a potential slowdown in demand and the need for corrective actions.

BUSINESS INTERPRETATION

The analysis suggests that the company relies heavily on key markets like Brazil while maintaining a diversified product mix.

However, the declining revenue trend signals a potential risk in demand or commercial performance, making it necessary to:

Strengthen demand planning
Optimize channel strategies
Evaluate promotional activities
